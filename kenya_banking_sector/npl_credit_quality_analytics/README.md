# 📉 NPL & Credit Quality Analytics

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Roadmap-blue)

## 📋 Overview
A specialized deep-dive into the Non-Performing Loan (NPL) crisis in the Kenyan banking sector. This tool provides granular analysis of credit risk across different sectors (Real Estate, Agriculture, Trade) and bank tiers.

## 🚀 Key Objectives
- **Sectoral Risk Profiles**: Identifying which industries are driving NPL growth.
- **Vintage Loss Modeling**: Tracking how loan cohorts degrade over time.
- **Provisioning Audit**: Comparing IFRS 9 expected credit losses vs. actual defaults.

*Developed as part of the Kenya Banking Sector Analytics Portfolio*
