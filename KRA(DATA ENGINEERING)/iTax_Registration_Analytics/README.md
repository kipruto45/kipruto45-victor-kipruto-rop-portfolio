## 📋 Overview
This project scrapes the KRA iTax PIN checker and business gazette notices to build a database of registered taxpayers. It tracks new business formation rates as a leading indicator of economic growth in Kenya.

## Data Sources
- **KRA iTax Public Portal**: Online PIN checker and taxpayer verification services.
- **Kenya Gazette**: Notices of business registration and corporate announcements.
- **Business Registration Service (BRS)**: Registrar of Companies records.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **PIN Scraping Pipeline**: Automated validation of taxpayer registration status.
- **Economic Indicators**: Tracking business formation by county and sector.
- **Geospatial Mapping**: Visualizing taxpayer density across Kenya's 47 counties.
