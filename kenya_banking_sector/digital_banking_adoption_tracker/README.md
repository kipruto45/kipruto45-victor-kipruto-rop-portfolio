# 📱 Digital Banking Adoption Tracker

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Research_Phase-orange)

## 📋 Overview
This project monitors the digital transformation of the Kenyan banking sector. It tracks the aggressive shift from traditional brick-and-mortar branches to mobile and internet banking channels, correlating physical footprint reduction with digital volume growth.

## 🚀 Future Features
- **Footprint Analysis**: Mapping the closure of physical branches vs. the expansion of Agent Banking.
- **Channel Profitability**: Comparing the cost-of-service between Digital vs. Physical channels.
- **Adoption Curves**: Segmenting adoption rates by county and demographic.

## 🛠 Tech Stack (Planned)
- **GIS Mapping**: GeoPandas for branch density visualization.
- **Ingestion**: Scrapy for bank footprint updates.
- **Analytics**: dbt for cohort-based adoption modeling.
